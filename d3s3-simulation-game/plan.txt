


Team Red/Team Blue

Phase 1: Making a normative case for Policy X/Y
- Policy question: Should Policy X vs. Policy Y be implemented?
Outcome: Brief presentation of case

Phase 2: Making an empirical case for Policy X/Y
- Provide groups with existing evidence on each policy (or let them do their own research; but then the question has to be precisely defined)
Outcome: Brief presentation / evidence pitch for each team

Phase 3: Adversarial collaboration for better informed policy-making
Next step: adversarial collaboration on intervention design to study which policy should be implemented
- Teams have to agree on study design: measures/KPIs, monitoring and evaluation design, decision criteria
Outcome: Brief presentation on design


Teilnehmer werden mit einer Policy Challenge konfrontiert. In Teams müssen sie Evidenz zum Impact mehrerer Policy-Optionen auswerten und präsentieren. Darauf aufbauend entwickeln sie ein Evaluationsdesign, um Erkenntnisse zu erwünschten und unerwünschten Auswirkungen der Policy zu optimieren und für Entscheidungen im policy-making-prozess einfließen zu lassen.
